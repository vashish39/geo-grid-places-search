Our Story

A gem worth the drive

Tucked along FM1625 in the quiet community of Creedmoor, Casa Cafe has become the kind of place people drive out of their way to find. A relaxed eatery where Tex-Mex tradition meets American comfort — from crispy breakfast tacos to cheese enchiladas smothered in housemade sauce.

Run by owner Jesse and a team that greets every customer like family, Casa Cafe offers dine-in, take-away, and even a drive-up window — because great food should be easy to get to.
